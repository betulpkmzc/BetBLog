Education

Antalya Bilim University

It is the second university and the first foundation university established in Antalya with the decision of the official gazette dated 21 July 2010. International Antalya University was established by the Gaye Education Health Sports and Environment Foundation. Rector of the university, which started admitting students for the 2012-13 academic year, Prof. Dr. Ismail Yüksek. It is Turkey's first and only international university. According to the Official Gazette news; 6 faculties (Economic and administrative sciences, Engineering, Tourism, Law, Communication and Fine arts and architecture), 4 colleges (Civil aviation, Foreign languages, Vocational school of Justice and Vocational school) and 2 institutes (Social sciences, Science sciences) will occur. Transportation to the school is provided by private shuttles and by public transport . The language of instruction is English. The school has students from 83 countries. It gave its first graduates in 2017. Currently, it provides services with a rectorate, two education buildings and a dormitory building in the central campus, and a preparatory department and a master's degree in the city campus. The girls' dormitory is in the central campus, and the boys' dormitory is in the district of Döşemealtı, where the school is located.

Department

Elektrical and Electronics Engineering

Electrical and Electronics Engineering aims to improve, test and supervise the design of electrical equipment such as radar, electric motors, communication systems, navigation systems or power generation equipment. Electrical and electronics engineers also assist in the design of automobiles and aircraft. Electrical and Electronics Engineers take part in the design, project, production and testing stages of the work. These people play an important role in testing the prototype, producing a new product or system, and at each stage of the project. Electrical and Electronics Engineering, which has a very wide field of study, is among the most preferred departments in terms of numerical score today.
