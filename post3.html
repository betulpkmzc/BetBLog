Series Suggestions

The Serpent

The Serpent tells the story of a serial killer who chases after Western tourists in Southeast Asia in the 1970s. Charles Sobhraj is responsible for the murders of 20 travelers traveling to various countries in Southeast Asia.

Who Killed Sara ?

Alex is accused of killing his sister. Therefore, he has to work to prove Alex right. At the same time, he is determined to take revenge on himself. Along with this effort to find the person who killed his sister, he will help reveal many secrets. He makes his first move towards revenge against the Lazcano family at the ceremony where Rodolfo is appointed CEO. There is also someone who wants to help him in this situation. A woman named Hunter Diana wants to help Alex and makes an offer.

Bozkır

Steppe tells the story of a superintendent and his crew trying to solve a mysterious murder. Seyfi is an experienced superintendent in his profession. Trying to solve a child murder with Nuri Pamir, a young and crazy rookie cop, Seyfi is dragged into a great mystery.

7 Yüz

In the 7 stories in the series, the characters face their darkest sides. The characters talk about the contradictions about whether they can ignore these dark sides and move on with their lives.

Behzat Ç. Bir Ankara Polisiyesi

He is a police officer who graduated from the Police Academy in 1985 and works in the homicide bureau. ... Behzat Ç. , is trying to solve the murders in Ankara

Yarım Kalan Aşklar

It tells the story of a man who dies as a result of an accident and returns to the world in a different body. Known for his bold journalism, Ozan and his colleague Elif have a happy relationship. The couple decides to crown their happiness with marriage . However, as they wait for their wedding day, a terrible event occurs. Ozan dies in a traffic accident. However, Ozan returns to earth in someone else's body. There are two missions waiting for Ozan, who is coming back to life. Ozan takes action to find out who killed him and to tell Elif the truth.
