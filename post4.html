The Best Comedy Movies

Project X

3 crazy high school students throw a big birthday party to get rid of their loser mood and have 'a little' fun. But as the night progresses, hundreds of people will flock to the party and things will get out of hand and they will keep their hands on the camera that has been recording all day long.

21 Jump Street

Two buddies, Schmidt and Jenko, who have just graduated from police school, are sent in disguise to a high school as trainee cops. Their duties here include alcohol, drugs, etc., involving high school students. While monitoring, reporting and preventing crimes, both are tempted by the lure of being a high school student. Because they will discover that the problems that they could not get over in their adolescence continue.

Why Him ?

Ned Fleming (Bryan Cranston) is a loving father to his daughter Stephanie. But her daughter has grown up and is of age to start her own life, and the day comes when the family finally gets to meet Stephanie's boyfriend. However Laird Mayhew (James Franco) is a very different character than they expected.

Let's Be Cops

On their way from Ohio to Los Angeles, these two men, who had all kinds of dreams but could not fulfill any of them, focused only on making a living, turned 30, disguised as cops to attend their school's homecoming party, one evening, and the entrance is o entrance.

Set It Up

Two young assistants working in New York literally save the lives of their boss. Assistants who work hard and do all the work for their boss earn very little and want to put an end to this situation.

Ölümlü Dünya

The Marble Family, which has been operating Anadolu Tat Restaurant at Haydarpaşa Train Station for generations, is a large family of 8 people. These people, who have a simple life on their own, live a very ordinary life from the outside. However, the truth is not like that at all.

Just Married

Tom and Sarah are an exemplary couple who are extremely happy to be together. Tom is a radio reporter and Sarah is a free-spirited writer, whose differences do not pose a problem for them, but draw backlash from their community. The couple get married in spite of their friends and family . They have good plans for the future and as a start they take a trip to Italy to spend their honeymoon, but those who are uncomfortable with their relationship will do their best to make their honeymoon hell.
