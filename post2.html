Life in Ankara

Education in Ankara

Ankara is home to some of Turkey's best universities and many world-class schools. From the opportunities these schools provide for those who want to go abroad to the simplest scholarship advantages, they are ideal schools for students. Students living in Ankara can easily benefit from this training.

Theatre

Ankara is a city with a very developed theater culture. Even someone who does not understand theater can always find halls filled to the brim. Maybe this ignorant person can finally improve himself.

History

Ankara has been the center of many historical events from the ancient cities to the beginning of our republic, and although it has not been taken care of, it is a city that smells like history and hosts many museums and most importantly Anıtkabir, where a student can visit and add something to himself. People living in Ankara have the chance to go to the Anıtkabir easily during special holidays and celebrations.
